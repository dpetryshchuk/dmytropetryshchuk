Hey there,

Welcome to my personal site.

My goal is to write as much of this code by hand as possible.

As you can see, it's a work in progress but I'm excited for where it could go.

I've started seeing the personal site as the equivalent of what the bike is to a car.

  • A tool to represent identity.
  • A tool that encourages autonomy.
  • A tool that expresses creativity.

It becomes a counter to the over-industrialization of the internet.

I'm hoping to host some essays, my thoughts, and we'll see what else.
